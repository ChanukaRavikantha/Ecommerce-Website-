About Us

Established in 1982 as a family business, our company began by crafting wooden chairs and settees. Over the years, we have evolved into a prominent supplier of school furniture, earning recognition from esteemed organizations like "Pasal Sanvardena Samithi" and "Gramodaya Mandala". By consistently delivering quality products, we expanded our clientele to include the IDB, Education Department, and Ministry of Education, both at the zonal and provincial levels. Through dedication and strategic investments in machinery, infrastructure, and human resources, we have grown to handle contracts from prestigious entities such as UNICEF and Save the Children. Presently, we are proud to serve all universities in Sri Lanka, alongside our extensive list of clients. With a modern workshop, ample storage facilities, a fleet of lorries, and a dedicated team of permanent and casual employees, supplemented by subcontractors, we continue to meet and exceed the demands of our valued customers.
