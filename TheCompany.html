About Sujith Furniture Manufacture

Introduction of Our Establishment

W.S.N.Fernando (The current owner) started this Company in 1982 as a family business with his father, brother & hisself, and made wooden chairs and settees inishiyaly. When we got orders from" Pasal Sanvardena Samithi" and "Gramodaya Mandala" to supply school furniture we got our business Registered on 015 of December 1983.

By year 1990 we were able to get orders for school furniture, through IDB and Education Department (Zonal & Provincial).By that time we had about 7-8 permanent employees. By Year 1995 we got the opportunity to get sub contracts from direct contractors of Ministry of Education in addition to the work we had.

By year 2000 we got direct contracts from Ministry of Education therefor we had to invest in new machinery, buildings and more staff.

In addition to what we were handling, in year 2004 we achieved another goal of handling UNICEF and Save the Children contractors in large scale.

At present we handle contracts of all the Universities of Sri Lanka, Ministry of Education, UNICEF.

Also we have many more modern machinery and other equipments, workshop buildings, store areas, 04 lorries and about 35 permanent & 20 casual employees and also presently we get the service from 05 Sub-contractors in order to facilitate the demand of our customers.
